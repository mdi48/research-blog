Null Ontology

What is Null Ontology?

Null Ontology is a project that aims to explore the concept of nothingness. It is a philosophical and artistic endeavor that seeks to understand the nature of existence by examining the nature of being (among other things).

What can you find here?

Here you will find a collection of articles, essays, and stories that explore topics such as phenomenology, horror, and (of course) ontology from various perspectives.

If you wish to learn more, consider looking into our Theory and Fiction
